# Sweet Miso
> Great on bread with butter.

#TK: Image

## Ingredients
- 300g Miso
- 6 tablespoons mirin
- 50-60g sugar
- 3 tablespoons water or sake
- 1.5 teaspoons vinegar

## Instructions
Stir all the ingredients together until the sugar has dissolved. This will keep in the fridge more or less indefinitely.
